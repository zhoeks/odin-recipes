Enchiladas Recipe

Description

This enchladas recipe is quick and easy and can be thrown together in a pinch whenever needed. The ingredients can be substituted for your favorite brand, or you can simply update with anything you like to accomodate your tastes! Enchiladas are my favorite Mexican food, and these have become the go to in our home

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 20 oz red enchilada sauce
  • 4.5 oz chopped green chiles
  • 8 soft taco flour tortillas
  • 1.5 cups cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375F. Oil/spray/tallow a baking dish roughly 3qt in size
  2. In skillet, coof beef over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes stirring until cooked, then drain
  3. Stir in 0.5 cups of the enchilada sauce and chiles
  4. Spread 0.5 cups sauce evenly in dish. Spread 0.25 cups beef in each tortilla and add 1 TBPS cheese Wrap tortillas and place in dish, topping with remainder of sauce and cheese
  5. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Wait 5 minutes before enjoying
