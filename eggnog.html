Eggnog Recipe

Description

Delicious and creamy, eggnog is Christmas favorite and this year I finally found a perfect recipe to make it ourselves. It uses pretty minimal ingredients, and does not take very long to make. If you've ever made custard or even homemade ice cream, you may be familiar with some of the steps making this even easier.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1-2 cups heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • Nutmeg to taste

Steps

  1. Begin by pouring your milk and cloves in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Beat the egg yolks until they start to lighten, then graduallyadd all the sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Temper eggs by adding up to a cup of the milk once it starts to steam, but prior to boiling
  4. Add the eggs/sugar/milk into the saucepan, and stir continuously until the mixture coats the back of the spoon
  5. Remove from heat before it boils and add the heavy cream
  6. Strain to remove and curdles or cooked eggs and cloves, and let sit for an hour
  7. Add cinnamon and nutmeg, refridgerate and enjoy!
