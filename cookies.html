Eggnog Recipe

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a family staple. Every home needs to have their signature cookie recipe for quick desert needs or movie nights or just something to have in the freezer when you need it. This is ours!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 0.75 cup white sugar
  • 1.25 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 0.5 tsp sea salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat ove to 350F
  2. Beat sugars and butter until smooth, then gradually add an egg at a time, then vanilla
  3. Dissolve baking soda in water, and add to batter with salt
  4. Stir in rest of ingredients
  5. Place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil, about 2 inches apart
  6. Bake for ~10 minutes, but check after 8 and remove if edges are brown
